Moral Values

Moral Values: They are the worthy principles that guide you to distinguish the right and wrong.

Honesty:being truthful and sincere.

Integrity:Sticking to your Moral and ethical principals and values.

Kindness: bring considerate and treating others well.

Gratitude: Showing appreciation for what one has and for the kindness of others.

Courage: The willingness to face difficult situations or challenges.

Empathy: The ability to understand and share the feelings of another person.

Fairness: Treating everyone justly and equitably.
